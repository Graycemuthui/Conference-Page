SAWASAWA FASHION WEEK 2022

SawaSawa Fashion Week is a yearly event that brings together creatives to meet, network and ofcourse gain more knowledge in the fashion industry. This happens in different countries each year. It was created with a purpose of letting each and every creative share their work and share their opinions freely. However this year we have decided to host it in the country where it all started i.e Nairobi, Kenya. We have decided to add more numbers to the guest list as you know for the past years we only had a particular number of guests. This year will be a new beginning as we target to bring in more ideal speakers and the program entails so much learning in the process.
